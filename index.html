Pyrography

...is the art of hand-burning designs typically into wood or leather.
If you like our style, we can do designs in other media, such as ink or henna as well.
We are a custom shop, so if you have an idea, or like what you see, get in touch.
We'll work with you to create something totally unique.

Our pyrography pieces make wonderful pieces of art for your home,
unique gifts for your loved ones, and memorable event decor.
We've done coasters, candles, spoons, boxes, coffee trays, and wall art, but the possibilities are endless.

Each piece is custom designed and hand burned, so average turnaround time is a few weeks depending on
the complexity and scale of the project.
